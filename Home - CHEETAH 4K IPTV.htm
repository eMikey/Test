What is IPTV
IPTV (Internet Protocol Television) is a technology that delivers television content over the internet instead of traditional satellite or cable formats.
How does IPTV work?
IPTV works by streaming TV content through internet protocols, allowing users to access live TV channels, on-demand videos, and more via compatible devices.
What do I need to use IPTV?
To use IPTV, you typically need a compatible device (such as a smart TV, streaming device, or IPTV box) and a reliable internet connection.
What channels are available with your subscription?
We offer a wide range of channels including sports, entertainment, news, movies, and more. Our channel lineup is constantly updated to provide our subscribers with the latest content.
When will I receive my order?

Which devices do you support?
We support all kind of devices such as smart TVs, streaming devices (like Firestick, Roku..), MAG, Enigma, smartphones, tablets, and more.
I purchased IPTV but didn't receive the credentials. How can I get them?
If you haven't received your credentials, please check your email (including spam/junk folder). If you still don't find them, contact our support team for assistance.
How can I set up IPTV on my device?

Do I need a satellite for using IPTV?
No, IPTV does not require a satellite dish. It operates over an internet connection.
